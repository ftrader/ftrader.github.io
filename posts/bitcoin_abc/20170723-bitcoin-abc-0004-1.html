Signatures of fourth Bitcoin ABC binary release (20 July 2017)

Linux, OSX and Windows Binaries for the 0.14.4 version have been built by myself and others using gitian (reproducible builds).

The binaries can be downloaded from https://download.bitcoinabc.org/0.14.4/ .
